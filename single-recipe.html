Banana Waffles

The aroma of these Banana Oatmeal waffles is irresistible. This is another quick hearty breakfast or brunch idea that can be quickly put together. Nice and soft on the inside, itâ€™s hearty and it has a very good flavor. They are so easy to make but they are delightful in their simplicity.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : bannana breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Add all the ingredients inside the blender and blend until smooth. Leave the batter to rest for about a minute before cooking so that the oat can have enough time to absorb the liquid.

step 2

Preheat the waffle griddle, and brush the griddle with some oil or simply spray with a non-stick cooking spray.

step 3

Pour the batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden brown. Serve hot with honey, maple, syrup, or favorite fruits.

ingredients

2 + 1/2 Cups Rolled Oats

2 Bannanas (Very Ripe)

3/4 Cup Milk

1 Tsp. Cinnamon

2 Eggs

2 Tbsp. Coconut Oil

1 1 Tbsp. Vanilla Extract

4 Tbsp. Honey

1 Tsp. Baking Powder

1 Tsp. Salt

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
